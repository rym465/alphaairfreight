Our Services

Alpha Air Freight Carrier is a premiere trucking and warehouse company located in Charlotte, NC and serving the greater Piedmont Area. Our customer base consists of international and domestic freight forwarders, US Customs Brokers, airlines, road-feeder services, shippers and consignees. We specialize in local pick-up and delivery, along with regional line-haul service. Our full service warehouse services include long and short-term warehouse storage, airline equipment break-down/build up, ocean container loading and off-loading, segregation, re-palletizing and custom crating services. We offer a nightly line-haul service to and from Charlotte, Raleigh-Durham and Atlanta with multiple trucks each evening. Through our solid agent partner network, we ship nightly throughout the entire Southeast. Our high standard of customer service and dedication to excellence for each customer is our on-going commitment. Alpha Air Freight has two facilities, Charlotte and Durham, NC. Our fleet of trucks consists of 53 foot tractor trailers, 26 foot lift-gate straight trucks and cargo vans.
